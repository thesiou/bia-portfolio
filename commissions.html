Commission Process

1

Initial Contact

Reach out with your idea, references, and any specific requirements. I'll get back to you within 1-2 days.

2

Quote & Agreement

I'll provide a quote and timeline. Once approved, you'll pay a 50% deposit to secure your slot.

3

Sketch & Approval

I'll create initial sketches for your review. This is when we finalize the composition and details.

4

Final Artwork

After sketch approval, I'll complete the final piece. Once you approve, you pay the remaining 50% and receive your files!
