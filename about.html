About Me

Hi, I'm Bianca Banu, a digital artist specializing in illustrations, graphic design, and animation. I'm passionate about creating vibrant characters and bringing stories to life through art.

With a focus on character design and dynamic compositions, I love exploring different styles and techniques to create unique and engaging artwork. Whether it's illustrations for personal projects or commissioned work, I put my heart into every piece.

When I'm not drawing, you can find me exploring new animation techniques, studying character movement, or getting inspired by other artists in the community.
